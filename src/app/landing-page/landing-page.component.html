Who Are We?

We are a team of passionate students who have experienced the ups and downs of navigating university education. We come from diverse academic backgrounds, each with our own unique set of skills and expertise. Our shared vision is to create a supportive community where students can find study partners, share knowledge, and enhance their learning experience.

A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia.

Why We Created This Website:

Having encountered the difficulties of studying alone and feeling the need for a platform that connects students with similar courses, we decided to build this website.

We believe that by connecting students who share the same courses, we can create a vibrant community of learners who support and uplift each other. We understand the importance of collaboration and recognize that studying together can be more effective, motivating, and enjoyable.
